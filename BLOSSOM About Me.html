About Me

Hola¡

Ellie Stevenson is a proud denizen of Christchurch, the greatest city in the South Island, possibly the world. She enjoys long walks in the park, a good book-and-cat curl-up combo, and an extent of hyperplanning that some have labelled worrying. Her work in the spheres of writing on current events and video analysis of storytelling is guided by the B.L.O.S.S.O.M mission statement:

  1. Block spoilers behind a warning.
  2. Leave AI out of it and let human contributors have their credit.
  3. Original is what you want to see. I’m not here to provide plot recaps - I offer real analysis.
  4. Spare no opportunity to spread knowledge about Aotearoa and te reo Māori.
  5. Sans smiles, a creator has nothing. I want to amuse and entertain and interest you in what storytellers can do better next time.
  6. Organic discussion about the real world and the narratives that flow on, not just talking points.
  7. Manual subtitles are a must if I’m to make the YouTube channel multilingual by voice and text for deaf and hard of hearing viewers.

If you believe in those ideas too, and want to have a kōrero, then click HERE to find out how to reach me.
